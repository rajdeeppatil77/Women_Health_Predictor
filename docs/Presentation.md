## Slide 2: The Problem
- Limited healthcare access in tribal areas.
- Lack of awareness about women's health issues.
- Language and literacy barriers.
- Delayed diagnosis of critical conditions.

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution
- **Accessible Web App**: Simple, icon-driven interface.
- **AI-Powered**: Instant symptom analysis.
- **Holistic Support**: Chatbot, Dashboard, and Emergency contacts.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features
1.  **Disease Prediction**: Detects Anemia, PCOS, Thyroid, etc.
2.  **Emergency Button**: One-tap call to ambulances/helplines.
3.  **Health Chatbot**: Answers basic queries 24/7.
4.  **Dashboard**: Tracks health over time.

---

## Slide 5: Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Responsive Design)
- **Backend**: Python Flask
- **ML**: Scikit-learn (Random Forest)
